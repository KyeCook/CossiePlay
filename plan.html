Mission

The CossiePlay website is intended to increase revenue and profit margins for CossiePlay by promoting the services performed by the company (copslay construction/selling & party) and allowing CossiePlay to be seen by a greater multitude of people hereby increasing new and repeat customers.

Success Evaluation

Success of project will be determined via increase in customers/party-goers within a 3month period after site contruction. Previous methods of advertisement will be kept consistant for the 3month period. Upon the conclusion of the 3month period sales will be counted and compared with that of the previous 3months.

Target Audience

Target audiences of CossiePlay are to be individuals within the years of 35-50 and thus the constructed website is to be aimed at this age bracket.
